About

Welcome to sonictones!

My name is Christofer Asplund, and I am a huge lover of all things music.
I created this blog as my fourth project with the Code Institute.

On sonicetones you will find reviews of the latest and greatest new music releases. Whether you are a casual listener or a die-hard music fan, I hope that you will find something here that resonates with you. I strive to provide honest and thought-provoking reviews that offer a unique perspective on the music that I am listening to.

But sonicetones isn't just about my thoughts and opinions, it's also a community for music lovers to come together and discuss the latest releases. I encourage you to sign up and join in on the conversation by commenting and liking posts. Let's discover and discuss new music together!

Thank you for stopping by sonicetones! I hope you enjoy your time here and find something that speaks to you. Whether you are looking for recommendations on new music to check out or just want to engage with like-minded music lovers, sonicetones is the place to be.
